About

Written By: Zachary Kai » Published: 1 Jan 2019 | Updated: 25 11 2025

Hi! I'm Zachary Kai, maker of Road Less Read. It's a book blog, review log, literary recommendation resource, and eventual zine-o-pedia. Here's how it works:

  • Find something you actually want to read, fast. No distractions.
  • Only books that've become pieces of my soul. Nothing bad or worse, mediocre.
  • Classics, obscure finds, bestsellers, new releases... Books as bridges. To ideas, culture, change.
  • My recommendations? Short, sharp, and useful.

Ready for a reading adventure? The road less read awaits!

About Me

I've been reading since...I started forming memories. Perhaps the library's best customer as soon as I graduated from picture books, they've been my favorite place in the world since.

I'm Australian, and ridiculously lucky and proud of our libraries. Go support yours! Get a card!

Photo Credits --- Left: Gizem Akkaya | Right: Roberto Pettinau

I've been tracking the books I've read since late 2019, and it's one of the best habit-forming decisions I've made. Not just so I don't have unexplainable bouts of dejavu! So I can provide useful book recommendations to friends, family, and you, but also memories. The person who read the book I just finished isn't the one that's reading the one in-progress now, and a still different one will read the next one. Books as bridges, back to our previous lives.

Review Policy

I welcome book review requests from authors and publishers! I love reading, being introduced to new books, and supporting the publishing community. Perhaps you've got something I could consider?

And it's not just books! I also review zines, and would love to hear about yours.

Reviews are published on this site, in the reviews section plus on my profiles on Fable, Goodreads, Hardcover, LibraryThing, Literal, Open Library, The Storygraph (and Netgalley if applicable.) I'm not on social media.

Before Requesting A Review

Format: I accept eBooks (ePub only) and audiobooks (only for non-fiction.)

Timeline: I read at my own pace and don't guarantee publication dates. Reviews are posted when complete.

Honest Reviews: I provide thoughtful, honest feedback. I won't post reviews for books I don't finish or can't recommend. If I don't have anything positive to say, I won't say anything at all.

Genre Preferences

  • Preferred Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Science Fiction, Non-Fiction, Young Adult
  • Willing To Consider: Adventure, Comics, Contemporary, Cozy Mystery, Folklore, Graphic Novels, Historical Fiction, Humor, Mythology, Poetry, Retellings, Steampunk, Short Stories (Anthologies + Collections)
  • Won't Read: Autobigraphies, Academic Texts, Anime, Biographies, Crime Fiction, Domestic Fiction, Drama, Dystopian, Erotica, Grimdark Fantasy, Horror, Inspirational, Literary Fiction, Magical Realism, Memoir, Middle Grade, Paranormal, Religious Fiction/Non-Fiction, Satire, Science Fiction Romance, Technical Manuals, Thrillers, True Crime, Urban Fantasy, War

Theme Preferences

  • Adore Books With/About: Diverse Experiences, Queer Characters & Themes, Spanish Bilinguialism
  • Enjoy Books With/About: Consumer Technology, Internet, Linguistics
  • Won't Read Books With/About: Animal Death/Cruelty

How To Request

Email me with your book's title, genre, brief description (2-3 sentences), and preferred format. Please include a sample chapter or link to preview the book before sending the full copy.
