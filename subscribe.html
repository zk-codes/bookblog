How To Receive The Newsletter

Want curated books and curiosities delivered straight to you? I send dispatches with recommendations/reviews worth reading, and curiosities worth examining.

  • Subscribe via email.
  • Subscribe via RSS.

How To Use RSS

It's how the web's meant to work: no algorithms, no tracking, just content you choose appearing when you want it. New to RSS? Here's how to get started:

  1. Choose an RSS reader. I use FreshRSS, but other solid options include Artemis, CommaFeed, Feedly, Inoreader, or NetNewsWire.
  2. Add the feed. Copy the feed URL above and paste it into your reader's "add feed" or "subscribe" option.
  3. Read on your schedule. New posts appear in your reader when I publish them. No emails, no notifications unless you want them.

Why Subscribe?

Because you deserve better than doomscrolling. There are still books worth reading and stories worth sharing. The internet is still a wonderful place.

I write about scifi, fantasy, young adult fiction, non-fiction and the occasional literary tangent. I share what moves me, what surprises me, and what makes me think.

If that sounds like something you'd read, I'd be honored to have you along.
