Books I Want To Read In 2026

Published: 27 Dec 2025 | Updated: 27 Dec 2025

As I identified a few months ago, I’ve long struggled with starting a million projects and not finishing them. This...also applies to book series and authors I've adored.

In 2026, I’d like to play catch-up: at last getting around to books that’ve fallen by the wayside, despite being keen to read them! They're organized alphabetically by genre.
