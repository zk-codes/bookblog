Submission Guidelines

To be included in the Zine Makers Directory:

  • You must make zines (print, digital, or both!)
  • You should have a personal site or online space to link to
  • That site or space should be somewhere folks can find your work or learn about you

Note: You don't need to be a prolific maker or have a huge archive. One zine is plenty.
